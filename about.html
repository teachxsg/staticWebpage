About TeachX

Brain child of two passionate developers

TeachX is a programming tuition center that uses the best ways of teaching to accelerate the students learning potential and make your kid learn at an exceptional rate. Tired of witnessing outdated practices and overpriced tuition centers, we decided to create a revolution in the education industry. Our vision is to provide students with a dynamic and engaging learning experience that equips them with the skills needed to excel in the ever-evolving world of technology.

Founder Story: Challenging the Status Quo

Our journey began after working in a traditional Computer Science tuition center, where we observed a disturbing trend. Outdated syllabi, ancient teaching practices, and exorbitant fees were hindering students' progress.We believed that learning Python, a fundamental programming language, should not be a lengthy and expensive ordeal without significant advancement.

Driven by the desire to make a difference, we founded TeachX with a fresh perspective. We aimed to create a tuition center that offers fair pricing, the latest syllabus, and leverages cutting-edge technology to empower students to learn at an optimal pace.

Operating Principle: Embracing Innovation and Continuous Improvement

At TeachX, we recognize the rapid advancements in technology and the need for adaptable teaching methodologies. Rather than adopting a static approach, we have designed a dynamic and evolving system to deliver the best possible education.

As engineers at heart, we optimize our teaching methods using a feedback-driven approach. Our workflow is built on the principles of input, output, feedback, and change.

We continuously collect information, iterate on our teaching techniques, and leverage design principles to enhance the learning experience. With TeachX, you can be confident that you are learning from the best educators, using cutting-edge tools, and benefiting from the most effective teaching techniques available.
