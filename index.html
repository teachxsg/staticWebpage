Project based approach

Learning through MAKING

In the contemporary educational landscape, it's crucial to deliver an approach that not only imparts knowledge but also imbues practical skills. At TeachX, we harnesses the power of project-based learning (PBL) to make education more engaging and effective.


Project-based learning forms the core of TeachX's pedagogical methodology. It provides a holistic and practical exposure to students, setting the stage for interactive learning, and building a robust foundation in programming.


The advantages of this approach are manifold. At TeachX, one-to-one learning is promoted, ensuring personalized attention to each student. More importantly, students learn by making. This hands-on experience ingrains the knowledge deep within their cognitive structure, making learning more effective and retention stronger. Moreover, lessons are recorded and fully viewable, providing students the luxury of reviewing them anytime, enhancing the learning curve.


TeachX not only strives to make coders but engineers. The integral aspect of being an engineer is embracing the process of trial and error. This encourages resilience and problem-solving abilities, indispensable traits for any budding programmer.

Capstone Projects

The Cornerstone of Learning at TeachX

At the end of each module, students undertake a capstone project, such as creating a Python-based calculator. This provides a real-world learning experience, further solidifying their understanding of the concepts.
